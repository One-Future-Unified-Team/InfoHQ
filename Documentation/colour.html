Colour

Users could also edit text colour in all InfoHQ too. You could edit all text colour in InfoHQ pages and websites. By default, InfoHQ text colour is green with footer is blue. You could change it to any colour you likes if you want.

In order to change InfoHQ text colour, please find this line in the body tags. You could then change color:green to any colour you like. Remember to changed it across all of your InfoHQ pages if you wanted to change it in the entire InfoHQ.


Background Colour

You could also edit background color of InfoHQ pages and websites. You could change the background colour of all pages into any languages of your preferences. The default installed background colour of InfoHQ is white and #f1f1f1.

If you wanted to change content background colour, please find this codes below in body tag. The default content background colour is white but you could change it to anything by changing background-color:white to any colour you want.

If you wanted to change the entire pages background colour instead of content, please find this code below in style tag. The default background colour is #f1f1f1 but you could change to any colour in your preferences. In order to change, please change background: #f1f1f1 into any colour you want.
