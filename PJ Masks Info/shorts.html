It's time to get your brain in gear! Go on an adventure through space with the #PJMasks and learn all about our solar system!

PJ Masks follows the thrilling night time adventures of three young friends Connor, Greg, Amaya. The friends transform into superheroes, Catboy, Owlette and Gekko, when they put their pyjamas on at night and activate their animal amulets. Together they embark on action-packed adventures, solving mysteries and learning valuable lessons along the way. Watch out night time baddies the PJ Masks are on their way, into the night to save the day! It aired on January 28 2020 in UK and January 29 2020 in France.

Characters

  • Catboy
  • Gekko
  • Owlette
  • PJ Robot

Moral

  • No moral or lesson is offered in this short.

Trivia

  • This is the only short to air in 2020.
  • In the French version of the short, PJ Robot's English dialogue and voice actor is left intact, while Catboy, Owlette and Gekko are voiced by their French actors with rewritten dialogue.
  • This is the first (and currently only) time besides music videos that the PJ Masks have rarely had any lines, leaving the narrator to do most of the talking.

Gekko helps Luna Girl deal with Motsuki's whims! It aired on August 30 2019.

Characters

  • Gekko
  • Motsuki
  • Luna Girl

Moral

  • Do not treat others like a baby; they will not like it.

Trivia

  • This is the first short that Motsuki appears in.
  • It is revealed by Gekko that Motsuki is supposedly not a baby, even though she clearly acts, and talks, like one.
  • This is the second time one of the PJ Masks is alone in their HQ. The first time that happened was "Catboy VS. Robo-Cat."
  • Luna Girl telling Gekko that he had done babysitting before possibly mentioned "Terrible Two-Some." This implies that Luna was presumably spying on Gekko while he was babysitting Kitten Boy and Chicklette during the events of the aforementioned episode.
  • Motsuki is the second villain to be invited into the PJ Masks HQ. The first was Luna Girl in "Moonbreaker." This is also the fourth time Luna was invited into HQ, the first three being the aforementioned episode and "Best Friends Forever," as well as the short "Moon Rocked." This is also the third time Motsuki enters HQ, as well as the first time she has done so in her insectoid form. The first two times were "Moon Madness" (her debut episode) and "Best Friends Forever," both when she was in her moth form.
  • This is the last short where Kyle Harrison Breitkopf voices Gekko before Benjamin Hum takes over, though he apparently already started voicing the character for the hero profiles during the PJ Masks weekend takeover.

In their HQ Rocket, the PJ Masks throw a surprise dance birthday party for Luna Girl. It aired on December 23 2019.

Characters

  • Catboy
  • Owlette
  • Gekko
  • PJ Robot
  • Luna Girl

Moral

  • Celebrating a friend’s birthday every year can make them smile.

Trivia

  • This is the first short to air in 2018.
  • This short shows that the PJ Masks were throwing a surprise party for Luna Girl's birthday. The previous three characters who had their birthday celebrated was Sorceline from Les Pyjamasques et le cadeau de Sorceline, Gekko and Night Ninja from "Catboy and the Great Birthday Cake Rescue." Unlike her picture book counterpart, Luna Girl received presents from the PJ Masks during her birthday, while Sorceline received nothing other than acknowledging that her motive on stealing is her birthday gift.
  • Gekko calls Luna Girl by her nickname, Luna, for the first time.
  • The PJ Masks HQ Rocket reveals to have a zero gravity mode button.
  • Roman Lutterotti voices Catboy for the first time, replacing Jacob Ursomarzo. However, Jacob Ursomarzo would still continue to voice Catboy for the rest of Season 2 and the beginning of Season 3, up until the episode "Best Friends Forever."
  • This short, along with the next short, may had taken place after the events where Luna Girl had reclaimed the Moon Crystal from the remains of the Mega Magnet to restore her Luna Wand. This may include that the short and "Play Date on the Moon" were produced when Season 4 was in production, even though Kyle Harrison Breitkopf was still voicing Gekko at the time.
  • It is shown that Luna was invited into HQ for the second time.
