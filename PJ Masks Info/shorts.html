Shorts

PJ Masks has many games and app for all PJ Masks fans to play and use. There are 10 apps in total for PJ Masks. A place to know more about each app of PJ Masks.


After arguing about which of them has to clean the streets, the PJ Masks must learn to share the hard jobs to stop Romeo. It aired on November 27 2017.

Characters

  • Catboy
  • Owlette
  • Gekko
  • Romeo

Moral

  • It's not good to let tasks such as cleaning up remain unfinished.
  • You should team up with your friends in any situation.

Trivia

  • In this short, Romeo uses his Multiplying Machine invention from "Catboy Squared."

After Romeo creates a device duplicating Gekko's strength, Gekko must use his other skills in order to stop the villain. It aired on August 25 2017.

Characters

  • Gekko
  • Catboy (brief appearance)
  • Romeo
  • Robot

Moral

  • You should practice more skills instead of sticking to just one.

Trivia

  • It is revealed that Gekko is bad at singing, although he is good at singing the Theme Song.
  • Romeo introduces his Strength Booster.
  • Owlette is the only PJ Mask who does not appear in this short.

Owlette and An Yu help a baby bird with his first flying lesson. It aired on July 18 2019.

Characters

  • Owlette
  • An Yu

Moral

  • No moral or lesson is offered in this short, but it does encourage viewers to assist others in learning how to do something.

Trivia

  • This is the first short that An Yu appears in.
  • This is the third time a PJ Mask member entered Mystery Mountain alone. The first is Gekko in "Flight of the Ninja." The second is Catboy in the short "Masters of the Mountain."

At the playground with Gekko, Armadylan has to learn to control his anger and avoid wrecking the playground equipment. It aired on March 18 2019.

Characters

  • Gekko
  • Armadylan

Moral

  • It is best to control your anger.
  • If you feel like you are growing angry, find something to distract you and calm you down instead of focusing on being angry.

Trivia

  • This is the first short that Armadylan appears in.
  • This is also the only short Max Calinescu voices the character. This is the first short where all the villains are absent.

Catboy must face his biggest fear to stop Romeo from taking over the world. It aired on November 28 2017.

Characters

  • Catboy
  • Romeo

Moral

  • It's good to face your fears.

Trivia

  • Romeo introduces his Countdown Timer.
  • Owlette and Gekko are absent for this short.
  • The quote "This could be the beginning of a villainous friendship" said by Romeo after zapping Catboy with the Countdown Timer is a reference to the line "I think this is the beginning of a beautiful friendship" said at the end of the 1942 American romantic drama film Casablanca.

Luna's moths refuse to answer her and follow her commands, so she turns to Owlette for advice. It aired on April 22 2019.

Characters

  • Luna
  • Owlette
  • Moths
  • Birdie

Moral

  • Be kind to others and ask them politely.
  • Always speak nicely when talking to someone.

Trivia

  • Birdie makes her returning appearance in this short since "Owlette's Feathered Friend." Only this time, she is referred to as "little guy."
